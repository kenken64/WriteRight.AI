Questions

How does the AI score essays?
WriteRight uses GPT-4o aligned to MOE 1184. Essays are scored on Task Fulfilment (10 marks) and Language (20 marks). Feedback references your actual paragraphs with specific suggestions.
Is it really aligned to MOE standards?
Yes. Our rubric follows MOE 1184 criteria for Situational and Continuous Writing — Content, Language, Organisation, and Tone. The same dimensions used in O-Level English.
What levels does it support?
Sec 1 through Sec 5 (O-Level track). JC GP support is coming soon.
Is my child's data safe?
All data is encrypted and never shared. Essays are processed for scoring only — not used to train AI models.
Can my child upload handwritten work?
Yes. Take a photo, our OCR transcribes it, and you can review before submitting for scoring.
How is this different from ChatGPT?
ChatGPT gives generic feedback. WriteRight scores against MOE rubrics with band scores, tracks progress over time, provides structured model rewrites, and has parent dashboards with student gamification. Purpose-built for SG English essays.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. One click in settings. No lock-in, no penalty. Free tier continues after cancellation.
