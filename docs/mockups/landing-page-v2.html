Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common parent and student concerns

üß† Customer Psychology - OBJECTION HANDLING: Parents have specific concerns about AI, data safety, effectiveness, and whether this replaces human teaching. Address these directly.
Is WriteRight safe for my child's data?
Yes. We're Singapore-based, follow strict data protection standards, and never share student essays or personal information with third parties. All data is encrypted and stored locally.
Will this replace my child's English tuition?
WriteRight complements classroom learning and tuition, not replaces it. Think of it as having a 24/7 writing coach that gives instant feedback, helping your child practice more between lessons.
How accurate is the AI grading compared to teachers?
Our AI achieves 65% agreement with MOE-trained teachers on band scores. While not perfect, it's consistent and gives immediate feedback that helps students improve faster through more practice.
What if my child tries to cheat with AI-generated essays?
WriteRight encourages original writing. We provide coaching and suggestions, but students must write their own essays. The focus is on learning and improvement, not grades.
How does the parent-student reward system work?
Students create wishlists of rewards they want. You set achievement targets ("Get Band 4 = new headphones"). WriteRight automatically tracks progress and notifies you when goals are met.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, no long-term commitments. Cancel anytime and keep access until your current billing period ends. Your child's progress data remains available even after cancellation.
